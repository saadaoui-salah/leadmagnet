# BiggerPockets Reply Templates

Templates for replying to Atlanta real estate discussions on BiggerPockets.
Goal: Demonstrate expertise → Build trust → Funnel to DM → Sell market report.

## Strategy

1. **Don't sell directly** in replies (gets flagged/downvoted)
2. **Provide genuine value** first
3. **Reference your data** to build credibility
4. **Invite DM** for detailed info
5. **Follow up** with market report offer

## Key Rules

1. **Never sell directly** in public replies
2. **Always provide value** first
3. **Reference specific data** to build credibility
4. **Invite DM** for detailed info
5. **Follow up** within 3 days
6. **Be genuine** — don't sound salesy
7. **Help first**, sell second

## Target User Profiles

1. **New investor** (0-1 deals): Needs education, starter report
2. **Small portfolio** (2-5 units): Needs market data, deal analyzer
3. **Active flipper** (5+ deals): Needs comp data, permit tracking
4. **Remote investor** (out of state): Needs turnkey analysis, PM recommendations
5. **First-time buyer** (house hack): Needs neighborhood guide, financing tips
